Humanity lives underground, isolated in individual pods, where all needs—food, communication, and entertainment—are provided by a global Machine. People have abandoned the surface of the Earth and any physical connection, relying entirely on the Machine for survival. One day, a man named Kuno warns his mother, Vashti, that the Machine is failing and urges her to visit him in person, something almost unheard of in their world.

As the Machine begins to break down, society collapses. People realize too late that they’ve lost the skills to survive independently. The story ends with the Machine stopping completely, humanity peris
