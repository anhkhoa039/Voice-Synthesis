A time traveler working as an agent for a Temporal Bureau recounts a bizarre series of events. The story follows a man who learns that he was born intersex, underwent surgery to become a woman, and later gave birth to a child before becoming male again. The time traveler recruits him and reveals the mind-bending truth: through a series of time-travel paradoxes, he is his own mother, father, and child. The story highlights the paradoxical loops of time travel and identity in a chillingly unique way.
